About Us

We are a team of people speaking up for LGBT rights, and inspiring people that need it the most, we educate each other. We have a lot of "straight" supporters who learn a lot about us too. This is community engagement, resource sharing, discussion, encouragement, on a global scale. Whatever your sexuality you can still support LGBT rights and do the right thing.

Our teams are here to help, we love to make a change and make someones day that little bit better.

Our promise

We promise everything you tell us will be kept a secret (confidentiality) thats important to us.

What We Do

We inspire people of the LGBT community, we give them support when they need it most. Best of all we spread love.
